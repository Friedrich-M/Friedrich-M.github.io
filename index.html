About Me

I am currently a third-year undergraduate student in the College of Control Science & the State Key Laboratory of CAD&CG at Zhejiang University. My research interest lies in the field of 3D Computer Vision, particularly including 3D rendering and reconstruction, as well as their applications in mixed reality and robotics. For more details, please refer to my CV. I am looking for potential summer internships!
